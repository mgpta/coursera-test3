THE MITHILA PAINTING

ABOUT

As we all know about the basic heritage and culture of art in the mithila region. The Mithila region mainly comprise of the districts of Madhubani, Darbhanga, and Sitamarhi. One of the popular myth about this region is that the Goddess Mata Sita, the daughter of Raja Janak, who was the ancient ruler of the kingdom of maithili(nowadays mithila along with janakpur(in Nepal)), borned here in Sitamarhi only.
The Mithila painting is the famous art of this region only. It is one of the most famous pinting in the world and is costly too.

PLACE IN SOCIETY

The current situation of this painting is that, it losts its identity throughout the globe. Actually, the local peoples or tribes who know how to draw it, couldn't get proper attention. Even on selling these paintings in local market, they couldn't get the proper amount of money which they acyually deserve.
Hence, the basic idea to globalise this painting throughout the world and getting its respect back into the society is only possible by letting people understand its value in the global market. For this purpose, a group of peoples formed this page to provide a global platform. This will not only globalise the MITHILA PAINTING, but also let people earn respect and employment also.
